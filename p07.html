Project #7 Autobiographical game

Game Concept

This game is showing my story with my dog, XiaoWu小五, who unfortunately died because of kidney failure last year. I want to use this game to record my memory with him. I always had the dream of being a witch and I would like to pretend that I am witch in Harry Potter and Xiowu being my spiritual animals. I also borrow the idea of pensieve from Harry Potter and used it as the way of telling my autobiographical story.

Design and Greybox
