Apple

Idea

In my first draft, I wanted to creat a recipe filter for those who live along. However, while searching for methods to achieve this, I found that it's too challenging to have a large enough database

Sketch & Process

After I decided to make website showing different cultural perspectives of apples, I startes to find the meanings adn symbolizations of apple in different culture, I refered a lot on an essay called "THE MOTIF OF APPLE IN DIFFERENT CULTURES AND ITS USAGE IN ANATOLIAN FOLK SONGS1" by Ahmet Emre Dağtaşoğlu and based on this article, I created these drawings I used for my website. I originally had two ideas, shown in the image below. First is to have a fixed background and let the apple change according to artistic styles of different places. Second is to have apple cutted into slices and each slice of them is representing a specific culture. In my final work, I basicaly combine these two ideas.

Reflection

I really enjoy designing the website without considering too much on porfessional side like typeface, layout and so on. I feel like I am just designing an artistic book with digital tools. :)
Something I wanted to improve more might be the interactive part. I want to include more animation and movement in this website. For example, on the main page, It would be nice to have sounds effect added while it is scrolled to the knife part. Or having some effect while clicking on the apple slices.
