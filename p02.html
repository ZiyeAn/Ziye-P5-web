Project #2 Multiple perspective

Apple

Idea

In this project, I wanted to explore different cultural perspective of an apple. I choose apple as my subject mainly because that is a fruit I think is normal but my mom always push me to eat. She never stop bragging how good it is to eat apples, so I wanted to figure out how poeple from the world view this normal fruit. I also include how I view it at the final section cuz I want also a private perspective of apples.
The childish and handwritten art style is specifically chosen and referenced on my childhood diary. I remembered even thhough I hated it so much but as a kid I have no power to reject eating apples so I could only curse apples secretly in my diary.

Sketch

I wanted to make a poem that is telling the story in linear narrative. Thus, I didn't design a lot of pages. Since the poet is using oak tree and ceiba tree as the metaphoric sybol for the position of two people in a relationship, I wanted to visualize that so I use hands as representations. Besides, I don't want too many words showing directly, so I want to make the veins clickable and showing the poem after clicking the veins.
I changed my idea a lot while making the website. I discovered that my original design can't really show the interacticity of the website. Thus, I decided to change from this linear forms of website into something that offers more options. The content of the peom leads me to think of the branches of the tree, i decided to replace branced with poem lines and thus enable users to click into these words and check the poem. I don't wanted to present all the lines and words directly on the website and I wanted more interaction so I check how to have words over images while hovering on w3 schools.

Reflection

I really enjoy designing the website without considering too much on porfessional side like typeface, layout and so on. I feel like I am just designing an artistic book with digital tools. :)
Something I wanted to improve more might be the interactive part. I want to include more animation and movement in this website. For example, on the main page, It would be nice to have sounds effect added while it is scrolled to the knife part. Or having some effect while clicking on the apple slices.
