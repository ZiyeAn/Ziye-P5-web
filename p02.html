Project #2 Multiple perspective

Apple

Idea

In this project, I wanted to explore different cultural perspective of an apple. I choose apple as my subject mainly because that is a fruit I think is normal but my mom always push me to eat. She never stop bragging how good it is to eat apples, so I wanted to figure out how poeple from the world view this normal fruit. I also include how I view it at the final section cuz I want also a private perspective of apples.
The childish and handwritten art style is specifically chosen and referenced on my childhood diary. I remembered even thhough I hated it so much but as a kid I have no power to reject eating apples so I could only curse apples secretly in my diary.
I used only grid for my pages since I like how it is more fixed that flexbox. I utilized it for decoration and page-switching menu. In the home page, I used it to add stars on two sides of the apple slices. In those subpages, I use them to create a small page-switching interface at the very bottom. Since I wanted this website to be more childlike and diary-like, I decided to use as less text as possible and make all the subpages looks more unified in forms, so I didn't change the way I use flexbox a lot in each subpage.

Sketch & Process

After I decided to make website showing different cultural perspectives of apples, I startes to find the meanings adn symbolizations of apple in different culture, I refered a lot on an essay called "THE MOTIF OF APPLE IN DIFFERENT CULTURES AND ITS USAGE IN ANATOLIAN FOLK SONGS1" by Ahmet Emre Dağtaşoğlu and based on this article, I created these drawings I used for my website. I originally had two ideas, shown in the image below. First is to have a fixed background and let the apple change according to artistic styles of different places. Second is to have apple cutted into slices and each slice of them is representing a specific culture. In my final work, I basicaly combine these two ideas.

Reflection

I really enjoy designing the website without considering too much on porfessional side like typeface, layout and so on. I feel like I am just designing an artistic book with digital tools. :)
Something I wanted to improve more might be the interactive part. I want to include more animation and movement in this website. For example, on the main page, It would be nice to have sounds effect added while it is scrolled to the knife part. Or having some effect while clicking on the apple slices.
