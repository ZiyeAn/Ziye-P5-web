Design Process

First, I designed a journal in the form of an svg so that i could use the 'secret tool' provided by Munus, However, I met problems while converting the svg into p5.js code. Then with the suggestion of Munus, I switch to Figma and succesfully convert the drawing.

In order to reach the effect of pages being torn off, I created two layers of pages inside the journal so it won't look wierd when the yellow sheet of paper were moved away. The I try to find the group of code that correspond to the part I wanted and then adding transltate() and rotate(). The most chaallenging part is the since the code is generated by the converter, it is quite hard to change the coordinates of the curves so that using translate and rotate together is not that successful.

Reflection

If the path could be more simply, it might be easier for me to find the group of codes I want and the coordinates of curves could be less for me to revise in order to use translate().Also, the effect of tearing the page of the journal is a bit of rigid. Hopefullig i could make it looks better after learning more.
