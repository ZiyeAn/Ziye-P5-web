Exquisite Corpose

Background Story
This is a cruel expriment done by aliens, they tried to modify themselves and to test how powerful they could be, they even capture angels and banana and include them in their experiment! They make banana alive by letting them eat a kind of sausages which are made of their family members. To see if merging their DNA with angel can make them stronger, they spent centuries to find the trace of angel, a race only live in the myth. They mercilessly skin angel and only keep their brain and skeleton and then, merge themselves with banana and angels.

I attempted to find tutorials on An Array of Images Processing Tutorial , but I still couldn't achieve the desired result.I also found another slot machine code on open processing which show the spinning effect by creating a array and put images show the interval of two objects and pretend that it is spinning.
